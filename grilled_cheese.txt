2 pieces of bread
2 pieces of cheese
enough butter to spread on one side of each piece of bread

heat skillet
butter one side of each piece of bread
place one piece of bread, butterred side down on the skillet
place both pieces of cheese on top of the bread in the skillet
place the other piece of bread, butterred side up on top of the cheese.
cook until bottom piece of bread is golden brown
flip
cook until the second piece of bread is golden brown
place on a plate
enjoy
